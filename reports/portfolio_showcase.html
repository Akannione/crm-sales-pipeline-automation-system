CRM Sales Pipeline & Lead Follow-Up Automation System

A Business Administration + CIST portfolio case study that turns messy CRM lead data into a cleaned pipeline, lead scoring model, follow-up queue, SQL reports, and management dashboard.

Core idea: Many small teams do not lose revenue only because they lack leads. They lose revenue because follow-ups are late, CRM data is messy, pipeline value is unclear, and managers cannot quickly see which opportunities deserve attention.

What I Built

  • Messy CRM lead dataset with duplicates, inconsistent labels, missing follow-up dates, and invalid values.
  • Python cleaning pipeline that standardizes data and deduplicates leads.
  • Lead scoring model based on stage, source, engagement, value, meetings, and recency.
  • SQLite reporting database and SQL analysis queries.
  • Follow-up queue for overdue, due, and missing follow-up leads.
  • HTML dashboard and executive summary for business users.

Business Value

This project shows how I can translate messy sales operations data into a practical workflow: clean the data, define the right KPIs, prioritize the next actions, and present the result in a way a business owner, sales manager, or operator can use weekly.
