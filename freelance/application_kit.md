## Short Proposal

Hi, I can help clean and organize your CRM or lead spreadsheet, remove duplicates, standardize fields, and create a simple dashboard so you can see pipeline value, overdue follow-ups, lead sources, and priority contacts.

I recently built a CRM Sales Pipeline & Lead Follow-Up Automation case study using Python, SQL, and dashboard-ready CSV outputs. The workflow cleans messy lead data, scores leads, creates a follow-up queue, and summarizes pipeline performance by rep, source, stage, and industry.

For your project, I would focus on making the output easy to use, not just technically clean.

Deliverables can include:

- Cleaned CRM/lead spreadsheet
- Duplicate and missing-field report
- Standardized stages and sources
- Follow-up priority queue
- Pipeline KPI summary
- Dashboard-ready Excel/CSV output
- Short recommendations document

## Stronger Proposal For Dashboard Jobs

Hi, I can help turn your CRM or lead data into a practical sales pipeline dashboard.

My approach:

1. Clean and standardize the CRM export.
2. Remove duplicate leads.
3. Create useful pipeline fields like expected value, follow-up status, and priority.
4. Build reports by source, sales rep, stage, and industry.
5. Provide a clear follow-up queue so the team knows what to do next.

I recently built a portfolio case study around this exact problem: a CRM Sales Pipeline & Lead Follow-Up Automation System using Python, SQL, SQLite, and dashboard-ready outputs.

The goal is to give you a report your team can actually use weekly.

## Client Questions To Ask

- What CRM or spreadsheet format are you using?
- What fields are included in the export?
- Do you already have sales stages defined?
- What counts as a high-priority lead for your business?
- Do you want a one-time cleanup or a repeatable reporting workflow?
- Should the output be in Excel, Google Sheets, CSV, Power BI, or another format?
