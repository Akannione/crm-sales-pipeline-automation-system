## LinkedIn Post Draft

I built a new portfolio case study: CRM Sales Pipeline & Lead Follow-Up Automation System.

The project focuses on a common business problem: teams often have leads, but the CRM data is messy, follow-ups are overdue, and managers cannot quickly see which opportunities need attention.

What I built:

- generated a realistic messy CRM lead dataset
- cleaned duplicate and inconsistent records with Python
- standardized lead sources, sales stages, dates, values, and email statuses
- created lead scoring based on stage, source, engagement, value, and contact recency
- flagged overdue, due-this-week, and missing follow-up leads
- loaded the clean data into SQLite
- used SQL to analyze pipeline performance by sales rep, source, stage, and industry
- created a dashboard and executive summary for weekly sales operations review

This project connects my Business Administration and Computer Information Systems Technology background with practical sales operations, reporting, and automation work.

Skills used: Python, SQL, SQLite, CRM data cleanup, lead scoring, dashboard design, KPI reporting, sales pipeline analysis, and business systems thinking.
